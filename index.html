About me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Department of Computer Science, Aalborg University. My research interests span AI for Spatio-temporal Data Management, and AI for Education. I have authored and co-authored more than 20 publications in prestigious journals and conferences, including VLDB, ICML, WWW, CIKM, and IEEE Transactions, achieving an h-index of 9 with over 520 citations according to Google Scholar.

I have actively contributed to the research community by serving as a Program Committee Member for leading conferences such as AAAI (2024–2026) and DASFAA (2024–2025), as well as IEEE EI2 2024 and BESC 2025. In addition, I have been invited to review for top-tier conferences and journals including TKDE, IJCAI, CIKM, EMNLP, and IEEE Transactions.

Research Interests

My current research interests include:

  • AI for Spatio-temporal Data Management
  • AI for Education
